WELCOME

De La Salle Dasmarinas Univeristy is open for enrollment.

The DLSU-D Admissions Portal is an online application facility where a student-applicant can fill out application form, upload pre-admission requirements, view the result of DQuEx (DLSU-D Qualifying Exam), and get admission status updates. This online application facility also serves as a tool for providing important information on the student admission process. The DLSU-D Admissions Portal account of a student-applicant shall be used for online application and admission slot confirmation purposes only. Be a Lasallian, apply today!
